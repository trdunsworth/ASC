Who We Are

Addiction Stress Center is owned and operated by Scott Robinson Jr. LAC. He has been counseling people with addictions for over 40 years.

He brings experience, care, and compassion to every patient because he realizes that healing needs a holistic approach and is willing to work with every person to give them the best experience and chance for healing and healing the families touched by addiction.
